Privacy Policy

1. Overview

At TaskFlow, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information in accordance with GDPR and industry best practices.

2. Information We Collect

We collect essential data to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, hashed password
  • Workflow Data: Task configurations, execution history, automation rules
  • Usage Analytics: Login timestamps, feature usage, performance metrics
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information (for security purposes)

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Account management and authentication
  • Providing and improving TaskFlow services
  • Sending essential service notifications and updates
  • Monitoring system performance and security
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or third-party marketing.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with secure HTTP-only cookies
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and principle of least privilege
  • Monitoring: 24/7 intrusion detection and security logging
  • Backups: Regular encrypted backups with geographic redundancy

5. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. Limited information may be shared with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, cloud hosting (under strict data processing agreements)
  • Legal Authorities: Only when required by law or to protect our rights

All third-party providers are vetted for GDPR compliance and security standards.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@taskflow.example.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Remembering user preferences

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser, though disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal or financial records may be retained as required by law

9. International Transfers

Your data is primarily stored within the EU/EEA. If transfers outside this region occur, we ensure adequate safeguards through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other GDPR-compliant mechanisms.

10. Children's Privacy

TaskFlow is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated via email with 30 days notice. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or data protection practices:

  • Email: privacy@taskflow.example
  • Discord: discord.gg/taskflow
