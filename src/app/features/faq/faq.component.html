Is my data secure? ▼

Yes. TaskFlow uses industry-standard security practices including JWT authentication, encrypted transport (TLS), and role-based access control.

  • All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication with token-based sessions
  • Regular security audits and updates
Can I cancel anytime? ▼

There's no auto-renewal. Subscriptions are month-to-month. If you don't renew, access will lapse automatically without charges.

  • No long-term contracts
  • Cancel or pause anytime
  • Manage everything from your dashboard
How long after payment until activation? ▼

Immediately after payment confirmation. You'll be redirected to the dashboard and can start configuring your workflows right away.

  • Instant account activation
  • Settings preserved from trial period
  • Support available if any issues occur
Is there a trial available? ▼

Yes, we offer a 24-hour free trial for new users. Trial access includes core features with a countdown timer and option to upgrade anytime.

When the trial ends, access stops unless you purchase a plan.

How do I get started? ▼
  1. Create an account and select a plan
  2. Configure your workflow rules and thresholds
  3. Set execution policies and filters
  4. Activate automation from the dashboard

No technical background required — intuitive interface with guided setup.

What can I configure? ▼

TaskFlow provides comprehensive configuration options for your workflows.

All settings are accessible via the dashboard with contextual help.

What is the system performance? ▼

The platform is optimized for low-latency execution. Tasks are processed within milliseconds under typical load conditions.

What execution rules can I set? ▼
  • Thresholds — set minimum and maximum values
  • Filters — include or exclude based on criteria
  • Scheduling — configure timing and frequency
  • Limits — prevent resource overuse
What payment methods are supported? ▼

We support multiple payment methods including cryptocurrency. Additional payment providers may be added. See Pricing for current options.

All payments are processed by secure third-party gateways.

What is the refund policy? ▼

Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis per our Terms of Service. Contact support for assistance.

How do I manage my access? ▼

Authentication is managed through secure JWT tokens. Access can be revoked anytime from your profile settings.

Best practice: Rotate your credentials regularly and enable two-factor authentication when available.

Can I access from multiple devices? ▼

Yes, you can log in from multiple devices to monitor and manage your workflows.

What happens during maintenance? ▼

Scheduled maintenance is announced in advance. The system pauses tasks safely and resumes automatically when online.

Which platforms are supported? ▼

TaskFlow runs in modern web browsers on desktop and mobile. Desktop is recommended for configuration.

What data is collected? ▼
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Workflow configuration and execution history
  • Performance metrics and system logs

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What is the uptime and support availability? ▼

The platform is designed for high availability with monitoring and automated alerts.

Support is available via Discord and support channels. Response times vary by plan and issue severity.

Are there usage limits? ▼

Fair-use policies ensure system stability. Plan-specific limits are listed on the Pricing page.

How often are updates released? ▼

Continuous deployment includes performance improvements, new features, and security patches. Major updates are announced in the dashboard.

Is TaskFlow a production service? ▼
This is a portfolio demonstration project. It does not connect to or interact with any third-party services.
